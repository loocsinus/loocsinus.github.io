About Us

Zhengzheng (John) Sun D.D.S.

Dr. Sun graduated from UCLA School of Dentistry with honors and completed a general practice residency at UCLA. He later served as a part-time faculty member, specializing in treating special needs patients.

Currently, Dr. Sun maintains hospital privileges at the City of Hope, enabling him to provide care for patients who cannot be treated in a conventional dental office.

A local resident of Los Angeles, Dr. Sun completed his B.S. degree in Computer Science at UC Irvine, followed by a Master's degree at USC Viterbi School of Engineering before pursuing his dental career.
